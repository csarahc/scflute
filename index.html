About



Hello! My name is Sarah. I am a university student studying computer science and playing the flute is one of my hobbies. I started playing the flute since the age of 9 when I joined band in middle school. From then on, I developed a strong passion for the flute and continued to play in band all the way through high school. I first started recording flute videos and posting them in 9th grade. I love musicals, classic, pop, and many more genres of music! I was also a first chair flute in Wind Ensemble during my junior year in high school. I enjoy playing and recording flute during my spare time in college and posting videos on my YouTube channel!
